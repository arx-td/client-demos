Fees Information

2026–2027 Fees

Termly fees for the academic year 2026–2027 can be found in the table below:

Area of SchoolAnnual FeeTermly Fee

Registration fee
£100 one-time fee. Covers processing of your child's enrolment and academic file. Non-refundable.
Enrolment deposit
£250, credited to your first invoice. Secures your child's place for 2026–2027. Non-refundable — this allows us to guarantee the place and plan class sizes with certainty.
Annual payment plan
One payment before the academic year begins. A 5% discount is applied to your annual tuition fee.
Termly payment plan
Three payments spread across the year. Each payment is due before the start of its term.

Enrolling more than one child? Sibling discounts apply automatically on the Annual Payment Plan, per additional child enrolled:

  • 5% discount for the second child
  • 10% discount for each additional child (third child onwards)
